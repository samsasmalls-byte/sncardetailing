Pricing & Payment
Pricing starts at $60 for an exterior wash, $80 for an interior detail, and $120 for a full detail. Pricing varies slightly based on vehicle size. Check our Services page for the full breakdown.
We accept cash, Venmo, Zelle, and PayPal. Payment is due upon completion of service.
Trust & Safety
Before every service we do a full walkthrough of your vehicle and photograph any pre-existing damage. You'll sign a brief waiver acknowledging the condition of the car. In the rare event we do cause damage, we take responsibility and do what we can to make it right.
You don't need to be present the whole time, but we do ask that you're available at the start for the pre-service inspection and at the end to inspect the finished result before we leave.
